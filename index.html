Wild Rift Meta

(Patch 7.2a) Wild Rift Meta Tierlist brought to you by GwenDiffYT.
Champions within a tier have no particular order.

Sign in to google or email to vote - only your email address and name (if on google account) will be shared to the database.
I have no use for your emails or names and won't do anything with it.

Please do vote if you want to help develop the tierlist; every vote matters.
When you disagree with a placement please vote up or down.
If you agree with a placement, please vote "Agree", this is as valuable as up/down votes.
When champions have hit over 60 move pressure their tier will be changed.

Contest Rating is a measure of how split people's votes on a champion are. The more disagreement, the higher the contest rating.

Move Pressure is a measure of how much votes agree on changes. The more consistent votes are for a champion to move up/down, the higher the move pressure.

Past champion tier changes will be listed, along with the votes and move pressure which brought them about.

Note: A and A+, along with B and B+ tiers, are similar tiers (little difference). The "+" tiers are only an extension of the tier to subdivide and allow more nuance.
Do not treat B+ tier as a completely different tier to B etc.

I hope this is useful for you, and thank you for visiting :)
